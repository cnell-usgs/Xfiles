Xfiles

UFO sightings package

This package provides direct R access to UFO sightings data from the National UFO Reporting Center.

Functions

UFOcount():

  • Returns the total number of UFO sightings for a given month and year.
  • Defaults to returning monthly counts for all years of data.

Example: ufos<-UFOcount()
ufos<-ufos%>%filter(ufos$year>=1950)

ggplot(ufos, aes(x=Date, y=UFOs))+geom_point(size=1)+
theme_minimal()+labs(x="Date", y="Monthly UFO sightings")

UFOobs():

  • Get detailed data on UFO sightings for given month & year or range.
  • Data includes Date + Time, State, City, Shape, Duration, and Description of the sightings.

Example: UFOobs(year = 1988, month = 10)

Date range: UFOS_2015<-UFOobs(year = 2015, month = 01, year_end = 2015, month_end= 12)

Warning: there is a high volume of UFO sightings data. It is suggested to first use UFOcount() to assess the volume of data being requested as it may take a while.
